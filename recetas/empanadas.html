Recipe to make empanadas

Venezuelan empanadas are crescent-shaped pastries, they are made from corn flour - which makes them distinctive from other types of empanadas - and any type of filling that can withstand frying. Not to mention that they are gluten-free empanadas, so they are appropriate for this type of intolerance.

Ingredients

  • salt
  • cheese
  • water
  • precooked corn flour
  • oil

Steps

  1. Put water, flour and salt in a bowl and mix until you obtain a homogeneous dough.
  2. Create balls of dough and then flatten them until they are as thin as possible.
  3. Place the cheese in the middle of the dough and close it. The dough should be in the shape of a half moon.
  4. Fry the empanadas one or two at a time until golden brown.
